# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## 1. Introduction

CareerPilot AI ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our job search automation service ("Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, password hash
- **Profile Data**: Target role, skills, work history, years of experience
- **Job Preferences**: Desired locations, salary range, job types
- **Resume Content**: Your master resume and related documents

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, timestamps
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system
- **Log Data**: IP address, error logs (anonymized)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Job Data**: Public job listings from job boards (via SerpAPI)
- **AI Analysis**: Job scoring and content generation (via Google Gemini)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Service
- Match you with relevant job opportunities
- Generate tailored resumes and cover letters
- Improve our AI scoring algorithms
- Send notifications about job matches
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **Supabase**: Database hosting and authentication
- **Google Gemini API**: AI processing (your data is processed but not retained)
- **SerpAPI**: Job search data retrieval
- **Vercel**: Application hosting

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

### 4.3 What We DON'T Do
- We do NOT sell your personal data
- We do NOT share your data with advertisers
- We do NOT use your data to train AI models beyond our service

## 5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS)
- Encryption at rest (database encryption)
- Secure authentication (Supabase Auth)
- Row Level Security (RLS) for data isolation
- API key authentication for integrations

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups**: May be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery
